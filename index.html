How Many Zones per Order?

58.6% of orders need only 1–2 zones. 23.9% need just 1 zone — a single picker fills the entire slot. 41.4% cross 3+ zones, and 27% cross 5+ — these are the complex orders that justify the putwall concept.

Single-Zone Completable Orders — by Zone

3F dominates single-zone completions: 1,336 orders (7.9%). These wall slots a single 3F picker fills entirely — no waiting on other zones. 2E follows at 924 (5.5%), 1G at 666 (3.9%).

Zone Presence — Solo vs. Shared Orders

3F appears in 65.7% of all multi orders (11,128). 2E in 62.3% (10,551). Green = that zone alone fills the slot. Blue = shared with other zones. An absent 3F picker directly stalls nearly two-thirds of all wall slots.

Top 10 Two-Zone Combinations

2E+3F is the dominant pair at 11.8% (1,997 orders). Top 10 two-zone pairs together cover 25.8% of all orders. Batching replen by zone pairs could pre-stage roughly 1 in 4 slots with just 2 pickers active.
